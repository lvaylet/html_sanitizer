Settings

Overview

You will display and edit the settings for Talend Studio. You will also understand how to use the help functionality.

Studio Settings

  1. To edit the Studio settings click "Window" then click "Preferences":

  1. Click "General" and "Perspective" and on the right side you see the list of available perspectives. Make the Integration perspective the default perspective and click the button Make Default and then click "OK":

  1. You see the active perspective on the top right and can also select it from there:

  1. You can always reset the perspective, click on "Window" then click "Reset Perspective" and then click "OK":

  1. The active perspective is also part of the menu, click "Window" then click "Perspective" to pick "Integration" from a list of perspectives:

Help

  1. To display the Help you click "Help" and click "Help Contents":

  1. The "Contents" including a search item will be displayed:

  1. The typical "F1" help button shows related help text. Select a Job in the "Job Designer" or click a component in a Jobnd click the "F1" function key and the "Related Topics" will be displayed on the right hand to the Palette:
